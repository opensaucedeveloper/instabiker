Our Frequently Ask Questions

Loan Faq

  • How much can i loan as a student ?
    As a student you are entitled to borrow up to NGN10,000,00

  • What is the interest rate?
    We apply a risk-based pricing to determine repayment on loans.

  • What do I need to be eligible?
    You must be in a paid employment
    You must have an active salary account
    you must have a business or a salary earner
    you must have a BVN
  • How do i apply?
    “Apply Now” sign up and proceed to apply for a loan in dashboard.
  • What is business loan?
    Our business loan will help you getting that business off the ground with a loan amount from NGN 500,000,00

Payment Method

  • How can I repay?
    You can repay with your card using paystack
  • Can I make part payment on my loan?
    No, you cannot make part payment as your loan repayment date are agreed before loan disbursement.
  • How much will i be charge when i make payment ?.
    You enjoy Zero transaction charge when using any of our payment solution.
  • Can i use my card to make payment ?
    you can start and complete your transaction using the paystack payment method in the system
  • Can i make payment befoe stipulated day?
    Yes you can. However ensure to notify us of this by sending a mail to customer@tactika.com.ng

Investment

  • What investment products does tactika have ?
    We have one investment product, Page investment notes. This gives you a high yield interest of up to 17%-20% on your investments.
  • What is the minimum amount i can invest ?
    The minimum amount is NGN200, 000
  • What is the minum tenor.?
    The minimum tenor is 12 months
  • Are there any associated charges
    There are no hidden or extra charges.
  • How can i get my interest?
    Interest payments can be paid into your account monthly, quarterly, annually or upon maturity
